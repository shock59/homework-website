Sound Effects

Here are sounds I recorded around the house, along with some contexts they could be used in in a movie.

Pencil

This sound could be used for someone sawing wood. It might sound more realistic if chunked out slower.

 

Chopping Onions

This sound could also be used as an effect for scissor chopping, rather than a kitchen knife.

 

Kettle

This sound could be used as a heavy rain background noise. This would be useful in a real life movie if it isn't raining when you need it to.

 

Ice Cube Tray Empty

This sound could be used as a sound for something smashing or crunching.

 

Door Closing

This sound is quite loud, so it could be used as a big boot stomping.

 

Cooking (Pot on Stove)

This sound could be used for rain, too, but light rainfall instead of heavy as the sound is quieter.

 

Blinds Chain

I couldn't think of a good use for this one, so I edited it and made it sound like a larger chain, which could be used for something like an old drawbridge opening.
